### 🚀 About Me

I'm **Akash Narayan** — an entrepreneur, political strategist, published researcher, and filmmaker based in **Bengaluru, India**. I founded and lead **[Legalit](https://legalit.ai)**, an AI legal operating system recognized with the **Best AI-Powered Solutions** award at the **NewsX India Business Awards 2025**.

Self-taught in code since age 13, my work spans **legal technology, artificial intelligence, and data-driven political strategy** — having advised major Indian parties (DMK, TVK, I-PAC) across successive Tamil Nadu elections. I also assisted Indian cybercrime cells in tracing an international peer-to-peer fraud network.

- 🔭 Currently building the **Legal Operating System** at Legalit
- 🌱 Seed investor in **sovereign-AI** startup Ayen (LLMs, multimodal, IDE, search)
- 📄 Published researcher — *[GUIs → Agentic API Ecosystems](https://akashnarayan.com/paradigm-shift.html)* (DOI: [10.14293/PR2199.002462.v1](https://doi.org/10.14293/PR2199.002462.v1))
- 🎬 Director of *D Love* (2026)
- 🗣️ I speak 8 languages · ✈️ Recreational pilot · 💬 Ask me about legal-tech, AI & startups
